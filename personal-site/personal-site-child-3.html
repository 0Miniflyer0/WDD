Mediterranean diet & Cardio Work

So what is the Mediterranean Diet? This diet consists of what is eaten at the countries that border the medditeranean sea. The countries, such as Europe, West Asia, North Africa, to name a few. So what does the Mediterranean Diet consist of?

According to heart.org: The Mediterranean Diet Consisted Of, Plenty of fruits and veggies, bread and other sources of grains, such as potatos. Olive Oil as a primary fat source. and Dairy products, eggs, fish and polutry in about low-moderate amounts.

So is the medditeranean diet heathy? Yes, absolutely. While i myself have never been on this diet personally. It puts an emphasis on eating: fruits, vegetables and grains. Includes low fat or possibly fat-free dairy, fish, poulty. And it does a very good job at limiting, sugar, and highly processed food, All the bad stuff.

We all know what Cardio is. Cardio, also known as Aerobic exercises, is when your heart beats faster and faster, and you starting breathing rapidly and then you start sweating. This form of exercise according to FYZICAL: "Is any form of activity that uses aerobic metabolism. That is, during the activity, oxygen is heavily involved in the cellular reactions that produce the energy necessary to sustain the activity." Some Cardio Exercises Include: Cycling, Running, Rowing Swimming. So what are some benefits to Cardio exercises?

Cardio is good for your heart, it decreases blood pressure and increases the base heartbeat at rest. Cardio helps increase blood flow and decreases chances of stroke. Cardio helps improve thinking and memory capability. It helps fight osteoperosis. Manages Arthritis. There's so many benefits to doing cardio exercises. It's almost no wonder that it's recomended for everyone.
